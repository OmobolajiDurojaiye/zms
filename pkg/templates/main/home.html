How ZITOPY Works

A simple story: From business setup to customer satisfaction

01

Business Owner Signs Up

A nail tech artist creates her ZMS account and easily sets up her business profile. She defines her services (like manicures and pedicures), lists prices, and configures her working hours.

  • Quick account setup for your business
  • Customize services, hours, and pricing
  • Manage your unique service catalog
02

Dashboard & Real-time Insights

With her ZMS dashboard, she gains real-time insights into her business: tracking product inventory (e.g., nail polishes), monitoring sales, managing upcoming appointments, and understanding customer preferences—all seamlessly integrated.

  • Centralized dashboard for key metrics
  • Real-time inventory and sales tracking
  • Manage bookings and customer information
03

Customer Books an Appointment

Her clients use a user-friendly customer app or booking portal (powered by ZITOPY) to browse her services, view real-time availability, book their appointments, and complete payments securely. Happy customers, thriving business!

  • Easy online booking for your customers
  • Real-time availability display
  • Secure payment processing
